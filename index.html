What is a Brain Tumor?

A brain tumor is an abnormal growth of cells in the brain. These tumors can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous) and can affect brain function depending on their size and location.

Risk Factors

  • Family history of brain tumors
  • Exposure to radiation
  • Genetic conditions
  • Age (more common in older adults)
  • Weakened immune system

Prevention

  • Avoid unnecessary radiation exposure

  • Stay informed about family medical history

Warning Signs

  • Persistent headaches
  • Seizures
